Zebee the Ancient Hen

She was a wise old hen.

The young hens followed her,
Every stubbornly slow step reminding them;
even the roosters respected her,
to avoid a violent peck
to the face.
The flock would tell you,
if they could speak,
better watch your step,
lest she put your life to rest in your sleep.

Zebee sure was one wise old hen.
But I bet,
those young hens,
are sure glad they'll never see her again.
